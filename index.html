Keto-Cheesecake-Recipes

Ingredients:

Crust (Optional):

  • 8 tablespoons salted butter
  • 1 cup almond flour
  • ½ cup coconut flour
  • 3 tablespoons golden monk fruit sweetener
  • **Optional: ½ tbsp almond extract

Filling:

  • 2.5 pounds cream cheese, at room temperature (5 packs) at room temperature
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated monk fruit sweetener (white or golden)
  • 2 tsps sour cream
  • ¼ cup lemon juice
  • ¾ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 6 large eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract

Topping (Optional):

  • 3/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup powdered monk fruit sweetener (add 1-2 tablespoons more if not sweet enough)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Supplies:

  • 9 inch springform pan
  • Tinfoil
  • Dutch oven or large casserole dish
